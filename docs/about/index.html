About

I’m a tech leader and software engineer who specializes in designing, munging, and shipping data.

Currently I’m a principal data engineer for Trello at Atlassian. Previously, I was a U.S. federal civil servant, serving as a tech lead and member of the engineering team at 18F, a consultancy inside the General Services Administration.

During my 18F tenure, I lead the technical implementation of the DATA Act, the nation’s first open data law. I entered the world of public spending via a role as the former Director of Data and Technology at National Priorities Project, a federal budget research organization.

A twenty-plus year tech veteran, I’ve held leadership positions in fields ranging from higher education to healthcare.
